@yudapearl An alternative encoding could be T --&gt; B --&gt; Y and T --&gt; Y, where B is the "chemical content of the drug." Then the "placebo effect" could be defined as the CDE P(Y|do(T=1, B=0)) - P(Y|do(T=0, B=0)), and the "real" effect as the CDE P(Y|do(T=1, B=1)) - P(Y|do(T=1, B=0)).
