And for readers who enjoy artistic science, here is a non-numeric counterexample:
M = X XOR U,   Y= M XOR U
This yields Y = X  and P(M|do(X))=1/2  and P(Y|do(M)) =1/2
So, the composition formula gives zero effect while the true effect is 1.
