Good question. Placebo effects should be encoded like any other effect, i.e., by thinking Nature:  T---&gt;PT---&gt;Y &amp; T----&gt;Y, where
T=Treatment, PT=Perception of Treatment. The research question is to estimate the direct effect of T on Y from the available data P(y|do(t)).
