@yudapearl I think the qualification “(absent temporal information)” is key. Time is often ignored or confused. If X is X(t), sometime Y is X(t+i) and sometimes it is Y(t). If we look at patterns in X, Y, *and* t (esp. w/ non-linearities &amp; deep networks) we can model causality for X, Y.
