Here is a simple puzzle for estimation experts: Consider the chain model 
X---&gt;Z--&gt;Y. Below are three valid estimands of E[Y|do(x)]:
1. E[Y|x]
2. SUM_z E[Y|x,z] P(z)
3. SUM_z E[Y|x,z] P(z|x)

Each estimand defines a consistent estimator if we decide to take
the MLE https://t.co/kMX5yvgn4h
