@thosjleeper @matloff Comments on your thread. The statement P(Y|do(x)) is causal but not counterfactual, because it implies no contradiction, and it can be estimated from RCT. P(Y|see(x)), on the other hand, is statistical, not causal, because we can evaluate it without experiments. #Bookofwhy
