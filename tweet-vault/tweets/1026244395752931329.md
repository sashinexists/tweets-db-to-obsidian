For Rubin’s approach, Pearl gives 3 assumptions:
•SUTVA — ur potential outcome isn’t affected by others treatment
•Consistency—taking treatment experimentally has same effect as deciding to take it
•Ignorability—potential outcome is independent of treatment given covariates
