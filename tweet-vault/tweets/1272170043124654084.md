@yudapearl @jodiecongirl The two criteria I’d put forward to distinguish inferences and predictions are: 1. The value you’re inferring is thought to be fixed at the time you build your model, a prediction is not 2. Uncertainty about inferences can shrink to zero; predictions are always uncertain.
