@hubertpaulo For "human thought" to be "governed" by causal logic does not mean that this logic governs ALL human thought. It means that whenever causal relations are at stake (eg 'helps', 'harms', 'affects') causal logic is AVAILABLE for the mind to distinguish the plausible from implausible
