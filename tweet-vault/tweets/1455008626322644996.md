The more I write about potential outcomes, the more I just want to call them "ideas" rather than counterfactuals.  I'm not David Lewis; I don't believe in the Multiverse.  Y^1 and Y^0 are just stories.  Yet in expectation, they teach you a lot of things you might not have seen.
