@AlloriMD "How" has two connotations: 1. How nature works, 2. How to achieve what we want. The former is perfect for mediation. The latter sits on rung-2. "Why" is often used to cover the former, as in "Why does lemon cure scurvy", which is almost synanymous to "How does lemon cure,,"
