@yudapearl Statistical mechanics (SM) and thermodynamics are both descriptive models. SM is from 1st principles, thermo is empirical. For certain weakly interacting systems (i.e. gas) one can derive from SM the equations of thermodynamics. Thus a math model that shows this asymmetry.
