@yudapearl Unbounded iteration cannot be handled by DAGs. On the other hand, cyclic directed graphs (or automata, as I prefer) can compute anything computable. It's the same for acyclic and cyclic networks, respectively. So cycles (or, more precisely, feedback loops) are important.
