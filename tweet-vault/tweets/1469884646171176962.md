@soboleffspaces Monotonicity is not experimental condition; it is Rung-3, saying Y(1,u)&gt;= Y(0,u) for all u. It cannot be ascertained from RCT data alone, because it asserts inequality "for every individual u". This is one example where the Rung 2 vs. Rung 3 distinction is important.
