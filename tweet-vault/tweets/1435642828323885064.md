@SilvioZaina There is a difference between hypothesis being "data-compatible" vs. "data-based". The germ theory of disease is "data-compatible" yet not "data-inspired". Ignaz Semmelweis (1818-1865)  had the data yet could not come up with a plausible hypothesis to convince his contemporaries.
