2/3) The variance-covariance matrix for outcomes, Y,  is V. Two vectors consisting of nm/2 values = 1/2 (treatment) and nm/2 values = -1/2 (control) describe allocation. Only the order of the terms changes: X1 is the vector for design 1 and X2 is the vector for design 2.
