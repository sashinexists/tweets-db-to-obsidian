Readers ask: Can there be "causal Inference" without an ESTIMAND?
Ans. I doubt it. I see many contenders to CI making causal claims w/o presenting an estimand so as to communicate the claim and verify its validity.
I would treat any such claim with suspicion; no CI w/o estimand.
