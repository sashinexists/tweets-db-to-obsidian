@ToKTeacher @DavidDeutschOxf Deep Bayesians apply Bayes rule to explanations, not merely to predictions. So, the question boils down to asking: "Why isn't Bayes rule adequate for finding 'best explanation'?". My answer: Explanations are Rung-3, ie, beyond the reach of probability theory.
