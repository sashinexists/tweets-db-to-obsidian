Whenever I see a Czech flag, I can't but remember Jan Masaryk, the Foreign Minister of Czechoslovakia, who agreed to send Israel 4,000 rifles, thus saving us from a genocidal attack in 1948. My basic training (1953) was still with Czech rifles. Masaryk Square now crowns Tel-Aviv.
