@widemberg @yudapearl A structural model is the data generating process for the outcome of the model, e.g., Y = f(A,X,e). If X are not children of A or colliders, E[Y|do(A=1)] - E[Y|do(A=0)] = E[E[Y|A=1,X]] - E[E[Y|A=0,X]]. PS methods are one way to estimate this quantity, which is the ATE of A.
