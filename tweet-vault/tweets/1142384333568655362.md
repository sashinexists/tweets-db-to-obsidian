@stephensenn Causal calculus does not "handle" things; it answers (1) research questions, given (2) assumptions and (3) data (experimental or observational). So far we got the question: (1) Find the causal effect of diet on weight gain, or P(gain|do(diet)). Now we need (2) and (3). #Bookofwhy
