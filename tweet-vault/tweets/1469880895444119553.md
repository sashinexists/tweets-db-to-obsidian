@artistexyz @soboleffspaces Identifiability is the ability to convert P(y_x) into any probabilistic expression, not necessarily P(y|x). For example,
P(y_x)=E_z P(y|x,z), or
P(y_x) = front door formula, or
P(y_x) = Napkin formula, or whatever do-calculus authorizes.
Exogeneity is just P(y_x)=P(y|x)
