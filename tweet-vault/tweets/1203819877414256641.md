@AndersHuitfeldt That's a gross misunderstanding.
 The Causal Markov condition is NOT assumed to hold a priori in nonparametric SCM. Labeling it NPSEM-IE (IE ="independent errors") is misleading. A notational system that gives you the OPTION to assume IE should not be labeled IE. #Bookofwhy
