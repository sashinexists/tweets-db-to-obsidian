•do(x) is an action. 
•P(Y|do(x)) is the population average outcome you “will see if you could do X=x” 

Compare to:
•Y(x) is the individual (counterfactual) outcome if you had done X=x
•P(Y(x)) is population counterfactual if everyone had X=x https://t.co/G5cXto7wN0
