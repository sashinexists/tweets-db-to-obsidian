CoRrELaTiON iS nOT cAUsAtiOn!

Correlation is not, by itself, necessarily indicative of causation. However, there's no causation without correlation. What matters is a plausible, testable, evidence-supported mechanistic connection. Dismissing correlations outright is nonsense.
