@soboleffspaces @causalinf @nickchk We are asking: what information must we have before the adjustment formula is valid. Ans. We must have (1) Rung-1 data to estimate the cond. probabilities involved, and (2) Some causal information coming from the graph (Rung-2). For example: Who are the (causal) parents of X.
