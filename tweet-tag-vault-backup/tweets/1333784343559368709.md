My memories are fairly muddy: Tents, mud, toys, mud, prayers, mud, food-line, mud, another rain?

 "Our lost brethren," mutters one teacher. "On eagle wings," echoes another. 

Where are they today, my brethren in mud? 
The tents are gone -- my brethren are my colleagues now.
